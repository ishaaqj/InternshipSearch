Help

  1. Create an account.

    1. If you are a new user, click on create new account and new user, underneath password on the login page.
    2. Now, choose if you are a student or a recruiter.
    3. If recruiter, fill all the details accordingly and click on submit button.
    4. If student, fill all the details accordingly. For adding fields like education, experience, skills, etc.
      1. Click add new Education/Experience/etc.
      2. Fill the details and click add education/experience/etc button to save that info.
      3. After finishing filling the form, click submit button.
    5. Now, you are the user of our internship website.

  2. Apply to a job.

    1. If you are a student, login to your student account.
    2. After you are logged in as a student, you can view and search internship jobs according to your preference.
    3. Then, you can click on the job, you are interested in and see the description.
    4. Click on the apply button at the bottom of every job description.
    5. Now you have applied to the job you were interested in.

  3. Delete a job application.

    1. In order to delete a job application, you need to login with your student login credentials
    2. Click on the jobs applied to button located in the dropdown list of the profile icon, at the top right corner, .
    3. Now, you will see the list of jobs you have applied to.
    4. Click on the job, you want to withdraw your application from
    5. Now click on delete application button at the bottom of the job description.
    6. Confirm, on the job delete application button by pressing dress
    7. Cheers! Your application from the job has now been deleted.

  4. Create a job post.

    1. If you are a recruiter, login to your recruiter account.
    2. Click on the create job post button located in the dropdown list of the profile icon, at the top right corner.
    3. Now, fill the details of the job post in the form.
    4. Click on the create job button at the bottom of every form.
    5. Now you have created a new job posting.

  5. Delete a job post.

    1. In order to delete a job post, you will have to login with your recruiter login credentials.
    2. After you have logged in, you will be able to see all your job posting.
    3. Click on the job posting, you want to delete.
    4. Now you will see your job description, click on the delete button on the tab in the right, just above the description.
    5. Confirm your delete, by pressing yes.
    6. your job post is now not available for the interns.

  6. Edit a job post.

    1. In order to edit a job post, you will have to login with your recruiter login credentials.
    2. After you have logged in, you will be able to see all your job posting.
    3. Click on the job posting, you want to edit.
    4. Now you will see your job description, click on the edit button on the tab in the right, just above the description.
    5. Update your job posting, accordingly. Now, click on the update button.
    6. Confirm your edit, by pressing yes.
    7. Now your job post is upto-dated.

  7. Check students who applied to your job post.

    1. In order to view students, who applied to your job post, you will have to login with your recruiter login credentials.
    2. After you have logged in, you will be able to see all your job posting.
    3. Click on the job post, you want to know about students who applied to your job.
    4. Now you will see your job description, click on the student applied button on the tab in the right, just above the description.
    5. Now you will be able to see all the students who applied to the particular job posting.
